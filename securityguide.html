Security Guide

Essential safety measures and preventive actions

Home Security

1

Secure Entry Points

Install high-quality locks on all doors and windows. Consider adding deadbolts to exterior doors and security film on windows.

2

Lighting Systems

Set up motion-sensor lights around your property, especially near entrances and dark areas.

3

Security System

Install a monitored security system with cameras and alarms. Regular maintenance is essential.

Preventive Measures

  • Keep doors and windows locked at all times
  • Don't hide spare keys outside
  • Maintain good visibility around entry points
  • Join or create a neighborhood watch program

Digital Security

1

Strong Passwords

Use unique, complex passwords for all accounts. Enable two-factor authentication when available.

2

Data Protection

Regularly backup important data and encrypt sensitive information. Use reputable antivirus software.

3

Network Security

Secure your Wi-Fi network with WPA3 encryption and a strong password. Use a VPN when on public networks.

Online Safety Tips

  • Don't click on suspicious links or download unknown attachments
  • Keep software and systems updated
  • Use secure, private browsing when possible
  • Be cautious with personal information online

Personal Security

1

Situational Awareness

Stay alert and aware of your surroundings. Trust your instincts if something feels wrong.

2

Travel Safety

Plan routes in advance, keep emergency contacts updated, and share your location with trusted people.

3

Emergency Preparedness

Keep emergency kits ready and establish meeting points with family members for various scenarios.

Personal Safety Tips

  • Keep emergency contacts easily accessible
  • Learn basic self-defense techniques
  • Carry personal safety devices when appropriate
  • Know emergency exits and escape routes in frequented places
