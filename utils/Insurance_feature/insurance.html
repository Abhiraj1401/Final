Frequently Asked Questions

What is covered under home insurance for natural disasters?

Home insurance typically covers damage from fire, wind, hail, and lightning. However, flood and earthquake coverage usually require separate policies. Check your policy for specific coverage details.

How do I file an auto insurance claim after a disaster?

Contact your insurance provider immediately, document all damage with photos, and keep receipts for any temporary repairs. Your insurer will guide you through the specific process.

Are there any specific health insurance policies for disaster recovery?

Some health insurance providers offer special disaster recovery coverage that includes emergency services, evacuation costs, and extended coverage periods during declared disasters.
